Mechanics Lab
Learning Approach: Concept + Virtual Work

Calculate the exact effort force required to lift varying masses using combinations of fixed and movable pulleys in a strict textbook style.

What you learn: The Conservation of Energy. Visually prove that pulling with half the force requires pulling twice the distance ($W_{in} = W_{out}$).
